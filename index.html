About

Xiaolin Han is a Post-doc Fellow at HKU, supervised by Prof. Reynold Cheng. Prior to that, she received her Ph.D. degree from The University of Hong Kong supervised by Prof. Reynold Cheng in Jan 2022, and received her M.Eng. degree from Waseda University, and B.Eng. degree from Sichuan University, respectively.

Interests

Her research interests focus on data mining and machine learning, particularly, on graph mining.
